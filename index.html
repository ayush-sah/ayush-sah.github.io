cat about.md

I'm a backend engineer focused on the kind of systems where correctness, observability, and speed all matter at the same time — financial platforms where a missed edge case becomes a real-world incident.

At Deutsche Bank, I've shipped three production systems end-to-end: a banking-API exposure layer (BaaS), an electronic invoice and mandate-based payments platform (Synthix Finance), and a money-market-fund trading portal (dbCIS) that I currently own from architecture through delivery.

My toolkit centers on Java, Spring Boot, Golang, and Python on GCP, with deep practice in microservices, Kafka, and CI/CD pipelines. I care a lot about clean APIs, structured logs, and tests that actually catch regressions.

Outside of work, I'm sharpening my data and ML chops — exploring Pandas, Scikit-learn, and applied analytics on financial datasets. I have an MCA from Sardar Patel Institute of Technology, Mumbai (CGPA 9.6/10).
